--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Music for Pieces of Metal
- balinese angklung version, for 9 musicians
- copyright (c) 2015, Pierre-Emmanuel LÃ©vesque
- based on Music for Pieces of Wood by Steve Reich
-
- symbols:
-   voice 0 -> 0 (metronome)
-   voice 1 -> x (ostinato)
-   voice 2 -> [a-h], z = last
-   voice 3 -> [1-8], 9 = last
-   voice 4 -> [A-H], Z = last
-
- instruments:
-   voice 0 -> 1 kajar
-   voice 1 -> 2 gangsa pemade (1 ngumbang & 1 ngisep), notes 1,2
-   voice 2 -> 2 gangsa pemade (1 ngumbang & 1 ngisep), notes 3,4
-   voice 3 -> 2 gangsa kantil (1 ngumbang & 1 ngisep), notes 1,2
-   voice 4 -> 2 gangsa kantil (1 ngumbang & 1 ngisep), notes 3,4
